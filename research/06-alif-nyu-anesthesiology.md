## PSLF Strategy
- NYU Langone IS 501(c)(3) — **PSLF eligible**
- 4 years residency = 48 qualifying payments
- +1 year fellowship = 60 payments
- +5 years academic attending = 120 payments → **full forgiveness**

## Key Timeline
- **PGY-1:** Intern year, take Step 3 early, set up IDR + PSLF, buy disability insurance
- **CA-1:** Simulation boot camp, subspecialty rotations, **BASIC exam in June (must pass)**
- **CA-2:** Senior call, explore fellowship options, ITE exam
- **CA-3:** Complex patients, Bellevue team captain, fellowship interviews or job search
- **Post-graduation:** ADVANCED exam in July, then APPLIED exam
